Who we are

What's Voyager 1 upto?

Voyager 1 is the first spacecraft which escaped our solar system using the method of gravitational assist.

Wait wait wait, Whats gravitational assist?

Answer is, it is a phenomena haha, wait let me explain you, just like if you keep a tenis ball over the basketball and let them free fall, you will observe that the tenis ball will bounce much higher than the basketball, but why, It do so because, all the gravitational potential energy that is converted in kinetic energy in the basketball get transfer in the tennis ball because of thier contact, hence the tennis ball bounces withh more energy. Making Voyage 1 escape from our solar system is the application of this phenomena.

Penumbral Lunar Eclipse is here!

May 5 will be a penumbral lunar eclipse respectively. Mark these dates on your calendar 🗓️ because it will be visible in Asian and European countries. A penumbral lunar eclipse takes place when the Moon moves through the faint, outer part of Earth's shadow, the penumbra. This type of eclipse is not as dramatic as other types of lunar eclipses and is often mistaken for a regular Full Moon. Click here for more details of penumbral lunar eclipse
